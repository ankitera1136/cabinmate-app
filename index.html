A PLACE OF HOPE

India's First Corporate Reading Room DRR has been re-branded into Cabinmate. At Cabinmate, we are pioneers in creating conducive environments for focused work and study with our Study Room and Co-working space solutions.

Originally launched as Deepas Reading Room in 2012 in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh we have grown and transformed into Cabinmate. Our expansion now spans 26 branches across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, reflecting our commitment to providing exceptional spaces for productivity and learning.

Since 2014, it has been expanding its reach through a dynamic franchise model, designed to bring our unparalleled reading room and workspace experience to communities across India. By partnering with us, franchisees gain access to our proven systems, comprehensive support, and brand recognition, ensuring they can successfully replicate our exceptional spaces in new locations. Our franchise network is a cornerstone of our expansion strategy.
